_Welcome _to Ample_23...

If you are looking for a place to shape your audiovisual ideas, you have come to the right place...

Looking for a place to shape your audiovisual ideas? Ample23 is your creative hub in Barcelona's Gothic Quarter. Our studio offers top-tier rehearsal rooms, music production, podcast facilities, and live session services. With a dedicated team of professionals, we're ready to help you transform your artistic vision into reality. Whether you're an artist, producer, or content creator, Ample23 provides the space and support you need to bring your projects to life.

Our workplace...

At Ample23, you'll find a fully equipped studio with high-end gear to support your creative process. We provide top brands like Orange, Blackstar, and Vox for amplifiers, Gretsch and Pearl for drums, and Shure and Neumann microphones for capturing pristine audio. Our control room and rehearsal spaces are designed for both comfort and precision, ensuring the best possible recording environment. When you're not in session, relax in our cozy waiting area, the perfect spot to recharge and prepare for your next creative breakthrough.
