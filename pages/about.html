How did Ample23 come about?

Ample23 was born from a shared passion for music and creativity in Barcelona's vibrant Gothic Quarter. The founders, a group of musicians and producers, envisioned a space where artists could collaborate and bring their ideas to life. What started as a modest rehearsal space quickly grew into a full-fledged recording studio, offering top-of-the-line equipment and services. Our goal has always been to provide a welcoming environment for both seasoned professionals and emerging talent, ensuring everyone has the tools and support they need to create their best work.
