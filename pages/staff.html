Meet our team...

At Ample23, our team is the heart of our studio. Comprising over ten dedicated professionals, we bring a wealth of experience and passion to every project. Our staff includes skilled studio musicians, who provide expert instrumentation, and coaches and professors who offer guidance and support to help artists develop their sound. We also have an experienced sound engineer specializing in mixing and mastering, ensuring that every recording achieves its full potential. Additionally, our audiovisual technician and studio assistants work behind the scenes to facilitate a smooth recording process. Together, we create an inspiring environment where creativity flourishes.
